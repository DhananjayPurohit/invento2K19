CENTRAL INDIA'S BIGGEST TECHNICAL EVENT

ABOUT INVENTO 19

Invento, The Official Techfest of IET-DAVV

is the biggest in the Central India. We are the students of IET-DAVV who organizes this mega-event every year giving an opportunity to the all technical enthusiasts to show their talent. In addition to this, Techfest aims at reaching out to all the students and promoting the vision of Technology and Science among the students. Year 2016, it ended up as a huge success when it witnessed the footfall of over 7,000 students from all corners of the Central India. Here are some of the glimpses of what was a thrilling 2 day experience for the visitors and the participants. Along with Seminars on Trending Technology like 3D Printing, IC engines, WireLess Robotics, Ethical Hacking and Construction Project Management by technical giants Mozilla and Microsoft Group, there were numerous competitions like Roborace, Hackathon, Quiz-Grey Carnage, Assembling-Disassembling and many more. Major Workshops were organized on Android app development, IOT and Cloud Computing. Students filled with enthusiasm participated in every event and fun activity and experienced unforgettable moments. Every time, we have a Motivational Speaker Session on the last day of this Fest. Last Year, renowned Mr. Swapnil Kothari made his benign presence to ignite the young minds which resulted to be the best finishing moment for this 3-day fest of amazement.
