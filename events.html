Doodle

Daydreaming or losing interest during class, scribbling during long telephone conversations with a pen on a paper are most often associated with young children.
A doodle is a drawing made while a person's attention is otherwise occupied . These are random and abstract lines, which are generally made without ever lifting the drawing device from the paper, usually called a "scribble".
