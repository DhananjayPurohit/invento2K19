About Us

Invento, The Official Techfest of IET-DAVV, is the biggest in the Central India. We are the students of IET-DAVV who organizes this mega-event every year giving an opportunity to the all technical enthusiasts to show their talent. In addition to this, Techfest aims at reaching out to all the students and promoting the vision of Technology and Science among the students.
